Visual Art

Visual arts consists of all artform that can be seen by the eye, unlike preformance art. Visual arts include but isn't limited to: photography, film, graphic design, and painting.

Traits Visual Artists Are:

  • Creative
  • Passionte
  • Resillient
  • Expressive
  • Hard Workers

Opportunities

Job opportunities for people with a degree in visual arts include: Illustrator, Photographer, Animator, Graphic designer, Arts administrator, Printmaker, and more.

Communications

Communications is a very broad major and can be anything from public relations to journalism. Put simply, communications is the study of human communication, weather that be through politics, entertainment, news or more.

Good Traits of a communications major:

  • Good writing skills
  • organization
  • Critical thinking
  • Passoinate

Opportunities

A communications may be useful because it can be applied to many different carreers such as a journalism, business, politics, entertainment and more! One of the most important take aways from a communications degree is the ability to effectivley communicate your ideas with others, which can prove useful in many different ways. Careers for communications majors include: Advertising copywriter, Digital copywriter, Market researcher, Multimedia specialist, Public relations officer, Science writer, and Translator amoung others.

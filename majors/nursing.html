Nursing

Nursing is a popular major that is gaurenteed to touch the lives of both the student, and the people they will go on to work with. Nursing requires a person who is physically, mentally, and emotionally strong. A career in nursing is demanding, but if you love people, are easily adaptable, are interested in helping and caring for others, and want to have an impact on the lives of others, nursing could be the right major for you.

Traits all Nurses Should Have:

  • Compassion
  • Care
  • Patience
  • Flexibility
  • Pays attention to detail
  • Physical endurace
  • Good problem solving skills
  • Empathy
  • Emotional Stability
  • High tolerance

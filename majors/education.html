Education

If you are well organized, patient, and love to be around people, persuing a career in Education could be the right choice for you! Whether you are interested in teaching young childeren how to read, or becoming a high school priciple, majoring in education can lead you to an exciting and prosperous career.

Important Traits of a Good Educator

  • Passion for learning
  • Good public speaking skills
  • Patience
  • Adaptability
  • Organized
  • Able to connect with crowds
  • Humilty

Teaching requires patience and a genuine love for people. Being able to connect with people of different backgrounds and learning to help them grow and develop is crucial to persuing a career in education.

International Relations

International Relations is a field that has a wide gamut of real world implications through jobs, international impact, and political work. With a degree in international relations, you can secure a job almost anywhere in the world, whether it's in a US Embassy overseas, working for the United Nations, or working in politics. International Relations is a major for people who are passionate about foreign affairs, and global issues.

Important Traits of a Good International Relations major:

  • Fluent in multiple languages
  • Passionate about global issues
  • Organizational and analytical skills
  • Okay with public speaking and working with people
  • Passionate about technology
    •

    Opportunities

    International Relations majors commonly pursue international or diplomatic jobs. Going into international relations can be very exciting for people who enjoy traveling and expireiencing other cultures. Some possible carrers for international relations majors include: UN ambassador, foreign service worker, diplomat, international business person, and more.

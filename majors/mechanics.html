Mechanics

Mechanics is a branch of applied mathematics and science that deals with mation and the forces that produce motion. A mechanics major will also study machinery and how the parts of machines work. To go into this field, someone should be interested machines and enjoy working with thier hands. Although mechanics can be a very broad major, one can study specific types of mechanics such as quantum machanics, or auto mechanics.

Traits Mechanics Have:

  • Customer Service skills
  • Communication skills
  • Diognostic and problem solving skills
  • Enjoys solving problems
  • Enjoys working with their hands

Opportunity

Job opportunities for people with a degree in mechanics: Auto or aircraft mechanic, repair technician, maintenance mechanic.

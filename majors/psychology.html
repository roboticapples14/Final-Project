Psychology

Psychology is currently one of the most popular college majors. If you think you might be intersted in persuing a psych degree, there are a lot of things to consider. A few important things to think about are how well you work with others, if you are well able to cope with stress, and whether or not you would be interested in furthering your education and attending grad school. A career in psychology requires someone who is analyitical and willing to commit lots of time to their job and studies. If you are interested in observing and understanding people and the way they think and act, psychology may be the right major for you.

Important Traits of a Good Psychologist:

  • Strong communication skills
  • Patience
  • Good listening and people skills
  • Analyitical skills
  • Observational skills
  • Problem solving skills
  • Trustworthiness

    Opportunities

    A psychology degree develops knowledge of human behavior as well as research, statistics, and writing skills. It can provide a solid foundation for many careers including psychotherapy or couseling, genetics counsiling, human resources and life coaching. After receiving a degree one can also return to school to get thier masters or pursue a doctrate degree.

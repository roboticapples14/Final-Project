Criminal Justice

Possible Traits of a Criminal Justice Major:

  • Good problem solving skills
  • Ability to see things from new perspectives
  • Open mindedness
  • Moderate physical ability
  • Pays attention to detail
  • Physical endurace
  • Good temperment control

Opportunities

There are many careers one can go into with a degree in criminal justice and many jobs to choose from. Some of these jobs include becoming a private investigator, police officer, FBI agent, corrections manager, or a crime scene investigator. They all involve working closely with others and being able to solve problems quickly and effectivly. And of course, one could always return to school to further their education and abilities.

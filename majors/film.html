Film

If you are a creative person that is good with technology, film and Videography may be the right major for you!

Good Traits of a film and videography major:

  • Creativity
  • Good under pressure
  • Good with technology

Opportunities

There are many jobs avalible to a person with a film degree. Some examples are Digital marketer, Film director, Film/video editor, Media planner, and Multimedia specialist. Film is a very versitile career as you can use it in combination with another one of your passions.

Business

Put very simply, business is the buying and selling of products to make a profit and there are so many ways to do that. To be successful in the business world, one should be a creative problem solver. However, because there are so many things to do in business, you doesn't have to be complteley set on what they want to do after graduating. Whether you aspire to develop your own company or want to make an impact on someone else's, majoring in business can be a great start in helping you achieve your goals.

Important Traits of a Business Major

  • Leadership skills
  • Competitive
  • Communication skills
  • Ambition
  • Reliability
  • Likable
  • Willingness to take risks
  • Takes initiative

Opportunities

Majoring in business opens a door to many fields and career options. One could become project manager, business advisor,investment banker, risk manager, stockbroker,supply chain manager or business analyst. A business major could even go into advertising or public relations. There are so many options and opportunities. Many graduates go into marketing or finance.

Engineering

Engineering is a branch of science and technology concerned with the design, building, use of engines, machines, and structures. There are many different branches of engineering including mechanical engineering, chemical engineering, aerospace engineering, biomedical engineering, and many more. Engineering is a very techincal and math-heavy major.

Important Traits of a Engineering Major:

  • Problem Solving
  • Attention to Detail
  • Good Communication
  • Good Math Skills
  • Logical/Critical Thinking
  • Creativity

Opportunities

Engineering majors often go on to do very important work. Depending on the specfic branch of engineering, there are many opportunities in many companies doing very important work around the world. An engineering major not only teaches students critical lessons about engineering, but it also teaches a valuble way of thinking and seeing the world. Some possible job opportunities include: civil engineer, NASA engineer, chemical engineer, electrical engineer, and more.

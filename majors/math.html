Math

Mathematics is generally a major chosen by people who are by nature analytical and approach problems thinking critically. Individuals in this major enjoy the concepts and formulas, and also enjoy learning to apply them to life's real situations. Thinking critically and understanding and definitely liking the equations and methods are also central aspects of individuals who choose to major in Mathematics.

Important Traits of a Math Major

  • Problem solving
  • Organizational skills
  • Ability to analyze & interpret data
  • Critical/logical thinking
  • Enjoy mathematical challenges

Opportunities

Math majors commonly pursue jobs such as Acoustic consultant, Actuarial analyst, Actuary, Chartered accountant, Data analyst/scientist, and Investment analyst.

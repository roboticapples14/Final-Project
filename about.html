Why Did We Make This Site?

We made this site to help other young people get a sense of what major they might be well suited for. As upperclassmen in high school, all three of us have college right around the corner and unfortunatley we still don't know what we want to persue in college! Now that we have made this website, we don't have to worry any longer. We are glad we got the oppurtunity to make this site in Girls Who Code!
